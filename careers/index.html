8 reasons to join Boon

2023 IS THE YEAR!

In 2023 we're reopening our office and building back up our team, while working on some super ambitious projects that are due for release this year. It’s an incredible time to join the company, and an opportunity to play a direct role in shipping best-of-breed mobile games to a massive global audience.

YOUR OPINION WILL COUNT

We're a small gaming studio with a team of less than 10 people. If you are an engineer, you will be working side-by-side with the artists and designers working on the game, and vice versa. You will get to experience all aspects of the game creation process from start-to-finish. Your actions will have an impact, you won't just be a cog in a larger wheel.

MINIMUM RED-TAPE

The entire team, including the founders, are game developers first and foremost. We love the act of creating games, and have a healthy dislike for anything that gets in the way. We keep meetings to a minimum - there's a quick daily catchup, and longer planning/design meetings at the onset of new projects, but for the most part, you will be able to focus on what you do best.

FOUNDATION, EXPERIENCE & BIG AMBITIONS

Boon is self funded with no external pressures. Past success has given us a solid enough foundation to go forth and create the games company of our dreams. We're focused on expanding our incredible core team to allow us to tackle more projects. We understand that great people make great games.

CULTURE

We have a culture of continuous learning, responsibility and ownership. We're a small talented team, trying to figure out how to be the best. We constantly ask ourselves how we can improve, and how we can produce games at the very highest level.

GREAT TOOLS

We won't skimp on the tools that allow our team to do its best work. We are continuously trying new hardware and software to improve our workflow. We're currently experimenting with Godot as a future engine, and several AI based workflows.

WORK IN-PERSON WITH GREAT PEOPLE

Remote work has its place, but in our experience, nothing beats getting a group of talented people working together in-person under the same roof. With the right people and the right environment, everything gets elevated a notch, and this allows for the creation of the best games possible.

DUBLIN IS A GREAT PLACE TO WORK

Boon HQ is on Fitzwilliam Lane, right beside the Merrion Hotel and within 5 minutes walking distance of Grafton St. It is one of the nicest parts of Dublin City Centre. Dublin is a vibrant city with lots to do and almost everything is close to the studio.

Life in Dublin

Ireland is known around the world for its stunning natural beauty, rich literary history, and strong cultural identity. Dublin values a healthy work-life balance and has a laid-back atmosphere, making it easy to relax and enjoy everything the city has to offer, from its bustling nightlife and famous pubs to its cafe culture, music, history, and outdoor activities.

Dublin is also home to a diverse and international population, and its growing startup and technology sector attracts some of the most brilliant minds from around the world. As a major travel hub in Europe, it's easy and affordable to explore other parts of the continent while living in an English-speaking country. Dublin has something for everyone, and there is much to discover and experience in this city.
